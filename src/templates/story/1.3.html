Planet System - Kepler 70c

Not a lot is known about the planet Kepler 70c, but it is one of the planets in the Kepler 70 system. It orbits its star very quickly. It was discovered along with Kepler 70b in 2011.

Planet Radius: 0.867 x Earth
Planet Type: Terrestrial
Discovery Method: Orbital Brightness Modulation
Planet Mass: 0.655 Earths
Discovery Date: 2011
Orbital Radius: 0.0076 AU
Orbital Period: 0.3 days
